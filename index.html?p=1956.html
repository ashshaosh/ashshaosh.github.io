IMPORTANT NOTICE: Launch this app before insert into IAA hosts.

Very simple reverberation effect for IAA and Audiobus.
You can create live and vibrant spatialisation with potentially endless fade-out tales.

You can change the “room size” and damp generated plume with certain frequencies. Every parameter can be randomised with 5 ranges, enable any or all at once with buttons above knobs.

Features summary:

  • Endless reverberations
  • Randomisers
  • Audiobus and IAA
  • Universal app for iPads and iPphones/iPods
  • Horizontal and vertical layout

Minimum required hardware is iPhone 4s, iOS 9.0
